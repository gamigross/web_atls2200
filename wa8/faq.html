Frequently Asked Questions

When does Camp Hope run?

Camp Hope runs two weeks every summer. The full 2026 schedule will be posted shortly. See Schedule for updated information.

How do I submit allergy and medicine information for the camper I'm registering?

Fill out the form found on the About page to start the registration process. Once you've submitted your contact information, we will reach out to you via email and ask you to upload a doctor's note PDF of your camper's prescriptions and/or allergies.

What should we pack for camp?

Sunscreen, bug spray, swimsuits, a waterbottle, and extra sets of clothes are recommended!
