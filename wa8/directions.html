Getting Here

381 Old Camp Road Bellvue, CO 80512

If you're traveling from Denver, you'll need to take I-25 N then exit at 269B. From there, you'll drive east on US 287 B, then take a slight left onto Rist Canyon Road, just past the Vern's Liquor store.

Turn right onto Road 41 from Rist Canyon Road. Take the dirt road up the hill, then turn right onto Tip Top Road. From there, you turn left onto Old Camp Road, where you'll see the sign for Camp Buckhorn. Park in front of the main lodge to complete check-in for your registered camp attendee!
